### Key Concerns with Voice Assistants

1. **Privacy & Security**
   - Always-on monitoring and data collection
   - Risk of unauthorized data use and hacking

2. **Accessibility Issues**
   - Cost barriers and internet requirements
   - Limited language/accent support

3. **Societal Impact**
   - Potential job displacement
   - Over-dependency reducing critical skills
   - Cultural and language standardization

4. **Personal & Social Effects**
   - Social isolation and emotional attachment
   - Impact on children's development
   - Unrealistic expectations

5. **Security & Economic Risks**
   - Potential for fraud and cybersecurity threats
   - Market monopoly and targeted advertising concerns
